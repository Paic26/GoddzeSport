About

Goddz eSports was founded in 2018, and has several teams in the esports scene such as the teams like Fortnite, CSGO, Rocket league and Overwatch and Clash Royale.

We want to help people by bringing them to our team and help reaching the professional scene. We want to help the players also physically, a good advice is that they should not be sitting behind their computer 24/7 because that is not good for your body. So we suggest that you must exercise by going to the gym or running .
